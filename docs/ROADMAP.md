# NexaFi Product Roadmap

This document outlines the short-term, medium-term, and long-term enhancements planned for NexaFi.

---

## 📅 Short-Term Goals (1-3 Months)

### 🔔 Smart Push & Email Notifications
- Push reminders for upcoming bills expected in the next 3 days.
- Weekly summaries delivered directly to user inbox.

### 💰 Goal Contribution Ledger Linkages
- Auto-allocate transfers matching goal keywords directly to savings balances.
- Support logging incremental deposits inside goal pages.

### 🧾 Additional Statement Presets
- Add bank statement presets for other major banks and fintech accounts (e.g. Paytm, PhonePe, HSBC, Citibank).

---

## 📅 Medium-Term Goals (3-6 Months)

### 🔗 Automated Bank Syncs
- Secure bank sync integrations via open banking APIs (e.g. Plaid, Yodlee, or local Aggregators).
- Automatic fetching of daily balances and credit card logs.

### 🪙 Multi-Currency Accounts
- Support logging transactions in USD, EUR, GBP, etc., alongside standard INR accounts.
- Automated daily exchange rate conversions.

### 🤖 LLM-Powered AI Insights (Gemini / OpenAI)
- Transition from client-side rule-based previews to LLM chat models.
- Support natural language queries (e.g. *“Compare my food spending this month to last month”*).

---

## 📅 Long-Term Goals (6+ Months)

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Cooperative Budget Groups
- Shared household budgets where couples or roommates can aggregate balances and allocate joint expenses.
- Joint savings goal targets with split contribution options.

### 📈 Investment & Portfolio Trackers
- Connect stock brokerage records or mutual fund indexes.
- Visual net-worth charts showing cash balances, savings margins, and investment growth over time.
